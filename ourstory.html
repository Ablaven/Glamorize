Fall 2024

The Beginning

It all started as a simple university project - just another assignment to complete before the semester ended. Five friends in a dorm room, brainstorming ideas for a fashion tech startup.

Winter 2024

The Spark

As we worked together, something magical happened. We discovered a shared passion that went far beyond grades or deadlines. We saw how fashion in Egypt was losing its soul, and we knew we had to do something about it.

Spring 2025

Taking the Risk

That night, we made a decision. We took the risk. We decided to build Glamorize from the ground up, fueled by nothing but our passion and a shared vision. We poured our hearts into creating something that mattered.

Today

The Dream Alive

Today, Glamorize is more than just a project. It's our dream come to life. It's a community of young Egyptians who believe that fashion should be personal, meaningful, and connected to our roots. And we're just getting started.
